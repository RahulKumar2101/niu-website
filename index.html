About Noida International University (NIU)

Noida International University (NIU) was established under the U.P. Legislature Act No. 27 of 2010 and is recognized under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956. Proudly sponsored by the Maruti Educational Trust, NIU is committed to delivering world-class education and fostering a culture of academic excellence and innovation.

Located in Greater Noida, within the prominent educational hub near the Yamuna Expressway, NIU is set on a 75-acre lush green campus known for its modern infrastructure and distinctive architectural design. Its serene environment and state-of-the-art facilities make it one of the most sought-after private universities in India.

NIU has rapidly emerged as a leading center for higher education, offering over 100 programs through its 12 schools, including disciplines such as Engineering, Biotechnology, Law, Management, Medical Sciences, and Social Sciences. These programs are designed to meet both global academic standards and evolving industry demands.

In its very first NAAC accreditation cycle, NIU achieved an impressive Grade A+ with a score of 3.44 out of 4, making it the highest-rated private university in India under this autonomous evaluation by NAAC (an organization under the UGC, Ministry of Education, Government of India).

NIU is proud of its diverse and inclusive campus community, with students from over 64 countries enrolled across various programs. This global representation enhances cultural exchange and prepares students for international careers and leadership roles.

NIIMS features a 750+ bedded multi-specialty hospital, offering integrated clinical and academic training, and setting benchmarks in hands-on medical education.

Noida International University (NIU) Admission Process Online

Visit the official NIU website and go to the "Admissions" section to access the online application form.

Register with your email ID and mobile number to create an account and verify through OTP.

Fill out the application form with personal details, academic qualifications, and course preferences.

Upload required documents (mark sheets, ID proof, photograph, etc.) in specified formats.

Pay the application fee online through available payment gateways (credit/debit card/net banking).

After document verification and fee payment, you'll receive admission confirmation via email/SMS with further instructions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Noida International University UGC approved?

Is Noida International University UGC approved?

Yes, Noida International University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has been awarded 'A' grade by NAAC. The university is established under the Uttar Pradesh State Legislature Act.

What is the placement record of NIU?

NIU has an excellent placement record with over 200+ recruiters including Amazon, Deloitte, Tech Mahindra, and Wipro. The university has a dedicated placement cell that provides career guidance and placement support to students.

Does NIU offer online degree programs?

Does NIU offer online degree programs?

Yes, NIU offers UGC-entitled online degree programs through its Centre for Online Education. These programs are designed with flexible learning options for working professionals.

What facilities are available at NIU campus?

NIU boasts state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, a central library with digital resources, sports facilities, hostels, cafeterias, and a medical center. The campus is Wi-Fi enabled with smart classrooms.

What are the scholarship opportunities at NIU?

NIU offers various scholarships including merit-based scholarships, scholarships for defense personnel, and special scholarships for economically weaker sections. The university also provides fee concessions for outstanding sportspersons.
